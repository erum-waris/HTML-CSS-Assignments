ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on
creating machines that can mimic human intelligence.It enables computers
to perform tasks that typically require human cognitive abilities,
such as decision-making, problem-solving, and learning. AI systems
rely on algorithms, data, and machine learning techniques to improve their
accuracy over time.AI is used in various fields like healthcare, finance,
and entertainment,transforming industries by automating processes and enhancing efficiency.


AI TECHNOLOGY

AI technology is divided into two main types: narrow AI, which performs
specific tasks, and general AI, which aims to replicate broader human
intelligence. Machine learning, a key subset of AI, allows systems to learn
from data patterns and improve performance without being explicitly Deep
learning, a more advanced form, mimics the neural networks of the human brain
to process complex data. AI is powering innovations like autonomous vehicles,
voice assistants, and personalized recommendations. Despite its potential,
AI raises concerns about bias, data privacy, and the need for clear regulatory.
